I'm an ELLIS PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems, supervised by Prof. Bernhard Schölkopf, Prof. Fanny Yang, and Prof. Amartya Sanyal. Prior to this, I completed an M.S. in Statistics at ETH Zürich and a B.S. in Mathematics and Economics at Nanyang Technological University.

I'm interested in understanding privacy risks in machine learning and developing methods to mitigate them, primarily through differential privacy and machine unlearning.
